About This Project

For my project, I used Hinge data as a foundation to explore how people express themselves and connect online. In a unique approach, Iâ€™ve chosen to represent the data visually through the metaphor of a mouth, embodying the notion of conversations and dialogues that start with simple words but often carry deeper, complex meanings.

Through a blend of text, visuals, and data-driven insights, this project transforms raw, numerical data into a narrative about online relationships, connections, and self-representation. Each visual element, from the shape of the mouth to the movement of the text, aims to capture the multifaceted nature of these interactions and the emotions behind them.

By reimagining data in this way, I invite viewers to reflect on their own experiences in digital spaces and the layers of communication that define modern connection.
